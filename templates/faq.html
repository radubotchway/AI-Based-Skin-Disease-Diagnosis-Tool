Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the diagnosis?

The accuracy of the diagnosis depends on various factors such as the quality of the uploaded image, the description of symptoms provided by the user, and the capabilities of the AI technology used. However, we strive to provide the most accurate diagnosis possible.

Is my privacy protected?

Yes, we take user privacy seriously. We adhere to strict privacy policies and ensure that your data is securely handled and protected. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

How can I upload an image for diagnosis?

To upload an image, simply click on the upload button on our homepage and select the image file from your device. Follow the instructions to complete the process.

Do I need an account to use Dermaid?

No, you can use Dermaid without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your diagnosis history and receive personalized recommendations.

Can Dermaid diagnose all skin conditions?

Dermaid is designed to diagnose a wide range of common skin conditions, including cellulitis, impetigo, athlete's foot, nail fungus, ringworm, cutaneous larva migrans, chicken pox, and shingles.
